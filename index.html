About Me

Oluwatoyin Akinselure goes simply by the name Toyin. Toyin is a Christian, she loves God. For a career, she is in the biological sciences and is a student researcher who is passionate about protein research and drug discovery especially, drugs low in adverse effect and high in effectivity. Ideally, Toyin loves to read books in her free time, she loves to travel and visit historic sites. she loves nature in general, from blooming flowers to visiting zoos to watching sunsets. she has an eye for nature. On holidays, she likes to visit beaches and parks. Museums too, to admire works of art, paintings, mosaics, sculptures, carvings and pictures. In her free time, she also loves to engage in conversations and garner varying insights on topics that interests her. She's an ardent follower of God and seeks to please Him in all she does. She loves people and loves to spend time with loved ones.
